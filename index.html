CV

I build digital products that are not just functional, but intuitive and inclusive.

With over 6 years of experience in the field, I’ve shifted my focus towards health-tech and data-heavy SaaS products. I believe the most complex problems often require the simplest solutions.

My approach is deeply rooted in research. I don't just design screens; I design systems and flows that respect the user's cognitive load. Whether it's making a migraine app accessible during an attack or visualizing terabytes of financial data, my goal is always clarity.

The Process

Workflow

From first idea to handover, I keep the process transparent and collaborative.

01

Discovery & Briefing

We define the problem, the business goals, and the user needs. I ask a lot of questions to ensure we are solving the right thing.

02

Research & UX Architecture

Drafting flows, wireframes, and validating concepts early. This saves time and ensures the foundation is solid before pixels are pushed.

03

Visual Design & Prototyping

Creating the high-fidelity UI, defining interaction patterns, and building a scalable component library/design system.

04

Handover & Support

Preparing assets, documentation, and working closely with developers to ensure the implementation matches the vision perfectly.
